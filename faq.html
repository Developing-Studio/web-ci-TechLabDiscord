FAQ

What is this server for?

This server is for discussing and sharing tech related things. You can also get tech support fast from our members and verified experts.

How do I apply for partner?                                 

If you join our server, you will see #partner-application channel. Visit this channel.

How do I get tech support?                                 

1. Join Tech Lab Discord server.
2. Go to #tech-support.
3. Describe your problem specific as you can. (You may join Tech Support VC too.)
4. Ping @Tech Supporter, @Smartphone Supporter, @Computer Supporter, or @Expert.
5. Wait until supporters help you.
